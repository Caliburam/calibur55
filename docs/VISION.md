# Calibur Vision

## Why Calibur Exists

Most productivity apps help people manage tasks.

Calibur exists to help people become stronger.

We don't want users to simply finish checklists.

We want them to build discipline.

Build consistency.

Build confidence.

Become the person they want to be.

---

## The Goal

Create the world's best AI-powered self-improvement game.

Not another todo app.

Not another habit tracker.

A game where real life is the gameplay.

---

## Core Values

- Progress over perfection.
- Small wins every day.
- Discipline beats motivation.
- Consistency creates mastery.
- Real life is the game.

---

## Long-Term Vision

A user should open Calibur and feel like they're entering
their own RPG.

Every completed mission strengthens their character.

Every failure teaches something.

Every streak matters.

Every level feels earned.

---

## Final Mission

Help millions of people become 1% better every day.
